Frequently Asked Questions

Which smart home systems do you support?

We work with all major smart home ecosystems including Amazon Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit, and Samsung SmartThings. Our technicians are certified in installation and setup for most major brands including Ring, Nest, Ecobee, Honeywell, and more.

How long does a typical smart home installation take?

Most individual smart home installations (like thermostats or doorbell cameras) take 1-2 hours. More comprehensive systems like whole-home security can take 3-4 hours. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your day.

Do I need to provide the smart home equipment?

You have options! You can purchase your own equipment, and we'll install it for you. Alternatively, we can recommend the best products for your needs and provide them as part of our installation package. We also offer links to trusted products through our Amazon affiliate store.

Do you offer support after installation?

Yes! We provide a 30-day support period after installation where we'll help troubleshoot any issues that arise. We also offer extended support plans for more comprehensive assistance.
