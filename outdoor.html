Frequently Asked Questions

How often should water features be serviced in Arizona?

Due to our high evaporation rates and mineral-rich water, Chandler area water features benefit from quarterly maintenance and an annual deep service to prevent calcium buildup and ensure proper operation.

Are your landscape lighting systems energy efficient?

Yes! We exclusively use LED lighting systems that consume up to 80% less energy than traditional halogen landscape lights, while lasting 5-10 times longer in our harsh Arizona climate.

Do you offer maintenance plans for irrigation systems?

We provide seasonal irrigation checkups to ensure your system is operating efficiently throughout the year. Our maintenance plans include controller programming updates, sprinkler head adjustments, and system inspections.

Is power washing safe for all surfaces?

We use variable pressure systems and surface-appropriate techniques to safely clean different materials. Our technicians are trained to recognize which pressure levels and cleaning solutions are appropriate for stucco, concrete, pavers, and other common Arizona surfaces.
